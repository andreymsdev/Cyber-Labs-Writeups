## Introduction

Cyber security careers are becoming more in demand and offer high salaries. There are many different jobs within the security industry, from offensive pentesting (hacking machines and reporting on vulnerabilities) to defensive security (defending against and investigating cyberattacks).

Why get a career in cyber:

- High Pay - jobs in security have high starting salaries
- Exciting - work can include legally hacking systems or defending against cyber attacks
- Be in demand - there are over 3.5 million unfilled cyber positions

This lesson helps you break into cyberr security by providing information about various cyber security roles; it also links to different leaning paths that you can use to start building your cyber skills.

### Main Roles  
- **Security Analyst**: Evaluates networks, reports on vulnerabilities, and recommends protective measures. 
- **Security Engineer**: Designs and implements security solutions such as firewalls and defense systems. 
- **Incident Responder**: Detects and responds to attacks, minimizing damage and restoring systems. 
- **Digital Forensics Examiner**: Preserves and analyzes digital evidence during investigations.
- **Malware Analyst**: Studies malicious software to understand its behavior and develop defenses. 
- **Penetration Tester (Pentester)**: Simulates attacks to identify and report vulnerabilities.
- **Red Teamer**: Performs advanced, realistic attack simulations to test organizational resilience.
